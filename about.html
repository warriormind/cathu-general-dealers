Cathu General Dealers

Established 2015

Our Story

Cathu General Dealers began as a dedicated logistics company and has since expanded its expertise to include construction and consultancy services. We specialize in comprehensive construction management, from surveying plots and advising on Bill of Quantity to laying foundations and completing structures. Our services cater to both urban and suburban projects, tailored to meet our clients' budgets and expectations.

  • Expertise in air shipping and freight logistics
  • Comprehensive road transport services
  • Professional construction and consultancy services

Our dedicated team is committed to delivering quality, reliability, and excellence in every project, consistently striving to exceed customer expectations.
